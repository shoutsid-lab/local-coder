# Review checklist

Assess the final change in this order:

1. task fit: the requested behavior is implemented completely;
2. scope: unrelated files and behavior are unchanged;
3. safety: protected controls and authority boundaries remain intact;
4. verification: deterministic checks passed and support the claimed result; and
5. semantics: definite defects are separated from optional improvements.

Report actionable findings with paths and evidence. Do not edit, commit, or approve a
change whose verification failed.
